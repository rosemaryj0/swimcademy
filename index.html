About Us

Welcome!

We are a private swim school in Newmarket offering swim lessons to the Lifesaving Society standard. We teach swimmers (ages 3 - adult) new skills & use personalized lesson plans to work on their areas of improvement. Our certified instructors focus on stroke technique and getting comfortable in the water.

For more information on our swim lessons, please visit our FAQ page, or send us an email at swimcademy@outlook.com.

Swimcademy is a Lifesaving Society licensed & affiliated swim school. You will receive an official report card & sticker badge when you pass a level!

Lessons are taught in our 40-foot outdoor pool in Newmarket. It is heated and has a shallow & deep end.
