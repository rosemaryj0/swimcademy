About Us

Welcome!

Swimcademy offers swimming lessons to the Lifesaving Society standard. We teach swimmers (aged 3 - adult) new skills & use personalized lesson plans to work on their areas of improvement. Our certified instructors focus on stroke technique and getting comfortable in the water.

For more information, please visit our FAQ page, or send us an email at swimcademy@outlook.com.
