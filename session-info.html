Session Info

Swimcademy offers the Lifesaving Society: Swim for Life program, and general swim improvement lessons.

Lessons run during July and August from 8am-7pm at our pool on Park Avenue in Newmarket, ON.​ Sessions are 5 days long, from Monday-Friday.

You have the option of 30min or 60min sessions (we recommend enrolling younger children in 30min, and older children in 60min). You can also enrol 2 swimmers at the same level into semi-private.
